# 🔐 CipherCraft

**CipherCraft – A Simple Tool for Text Encryption and Decryption**

CipherCraft is a basic and easy-to-use web app that lets you encrypt and decrypt any text. Whether you're just experimenting with cryptography or want to add a bit of privacy to your messages, CipherCraft is a great place to start.

## ✨ Features

- 🔐 **Encrypt Text** – Turn your message into a coded format.  
- 🔓 **Decrypt Text** – Convert the encrypted message back into readable text.  
- 🧠 **Easy to Use** – Simple interface, no confusing settings.  
- 🖥️ **Runs in Your Browser** – No installations or sign-ups required.

## 🚀 Getting Started

Just open the website in your browser and start encrypting or decrypting text right away. No setup required!

> Perfect for beginners who want to understand how encryption works or anyone looking for a quick and easy encryption tool.
